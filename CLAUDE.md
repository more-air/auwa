# AUWA — PROJECT CONTEXT

*Last Updated: 24 March 2026*

---

## WHAT AUWA IS

AUWA is a cosmic being from the solar system WAWA who reveals the kokoro — the soul — within all living things through a magical light shower and telepathic communication.

In the age of AGI where IQ is free and commoditised, EQ has become the new competitive advantage. AUWA is the first daily EQ practice designed to build emotional intelligence through Japanese cultural philosophy.

**AUWA does not fix. AUWA reveals.**

---

## THE THREE PHILOSOPHICAL PILLARS

These aren't marketing angles — they are the foundation Rieko grew up with in Japan.

**Yaoyorozu no Kami** — Shinto concept that spirits inhabit all things. No divide between sacred and ordinary. This is the heart of AUWA — the light shower reveals the kokoro within ALL living things.

**Kido Airaku (喜怒哀楽)** — Four core emotions: Ki (Joy), Do (Anger), Ai (Sorrow), Raku (Ease). Beneath each sit nuanced sub-expressions drawn from nature metaphors. Users experience 4 clear emotions; the depth is felt, not studied. The "72 emotions" concept has been retired — Kido Airaku is the user-facing framework.

**72 Micro-Seasons (Shichijūni-kō)** — Used as ambient philosophy, not user-facing taxonomy. Life moves through constant micro-changes — that's natural and worthy of observation, not judgment. In the app: a subtle calligraphic kō stamp (atmospheric, tappable for the curious). The AI's reflections do NOT force connections to the current micro-season.

## WHAT AUWA IS NOT

- Not clinical (no CBT, no diagnosis, no coping strategies)
- Not gamified (no streaks, no badges, no push notifications)
- Not generic (not another AI chatbot with a wellness skin)
- Not a weather app (micro-seasons are philosophy, not forecasts)
- Not advice-giving (AUWA reveals, period)

## KEY DECISIONS MADE

- 4 Kido Airaku emotions user-facing, sub-expressions under the hood (not 72)
- Micro-seasons as ambient philosophy, NOT tied to AI reflection text
- Web app (PWA) first, wrappable for native later via Capacitor
- Free tier is unlimited core experience; paid tier (£6.99/month) unlocks depth, not access
- Illustrations are the most valuable asset — refresh them BEFORE building the app
- No vanilla/character-free version (the character IS the differentiator)
- Hotel B2B deprioritised to Year 2

## THE KOKORO MIRROR — CORE UX

Arrival (dark, calm) → User types freely → Light shower animation → Kokoro revelation (gradient + AUWA character + calligraphic stamp + AI-generated poetic reflection) → Optional journal → Shareable card → Saved to Kokoro Archive.

What users NEVER see: the number 72, micro-season names forced into reflections, any taxonomy, advice or "try this" suggestions.
