**One-liner:** "The first daily EQ practice for the AGI generation."

**Elevator pitch:** "In the age of AGI, IQ is free. EQ is the new competitive advantage. AUWA is the daily EQ practice for the AGI generation: an AI companion that helps you build emotional intelligence through Japanese philosophy. While AI handles your thinking, AUWA develops your feeling."

**Hotel B2B pitch:** "Your guests can Google anything. They can't Google how to feel. While business travel stresses their IQ, AUWA restores their EQ."

## TARGET AUDIENCES

**Primary: Emotionally intelligent millennials & Gen Z (25-40).** Seek meaning over metrics. Drawn to Japanese aesthetics. Distrust generic AI. Consuming: LofiGirl, Studio Ghibli, cottagecore, slow living. Underserved by Headspace (clinical), meditation apps (lack cultural depth).

**Secondary: Luxury hotel guests.** Seeking authentic cultural experiences. QR-based PWA works instantly, no download. Japanese hospitality (omotenashi) philosophy fits naturally.

**Tertiary: High-end collectible enthusiasts.** Growing market for authentic Japanese craft objects and designer toys. Seek 10-year development stories, cultural authenticity, artist connection.

## GO-TO-MARKET

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-8)
Build and soft launch Kokoro Mirror to waitlist. Social content: behind-the-scenes (Rieko sketching), seasonal wisdom, EQ teasers.
**Gate:** 500+ email signups, 100+ beta users, 70%+ completion rate, 10+ paid subscribers.

### Phase 2: Physical Validation (Months 3-6)
First figure drop. Pre-order de-risks production.
**Gate:** 80%+ sell-through in 2 weeks. If sold out, pursue Pop Mart conversations in Year 2. If doesn't sell, pause figures, focus app + hotel.

### Phase 3: Cultural Validation (Months 6-12)
Craftsman outreach (Rieko leads in Japanese). Exhibition planning. Hotel outreach + demos. Ukiyo-e print series.
**Targets:** 3+ press articles, 1 hotel deal (£25-75K), 10K Instagram followers, 200-500 exhibition visitors.

### Phase 4: Scale (Year 2)
Pop Mart with sell-through proof. Hotel chains with working demo. Expand Ukiyo-e series. Seasonal music album on Spotify.

## DECISION FRAMEWORKS

### Week 8 Gate
**100+ users, 70%+ completion, 10+ paid:** Full speed. Invest in figures, accelerate hotel outreach.
**50-99 users, 40-69% completion, 1-9 paid:** Iterate. Improve onboarding, delay figures 1-2 months.
**<50 users, <40% completion, 0 paid:** Major pivot. Reassess concept. Consider hotel B2B as primary.

## STRATEGIC LESSONS

**KAWS:** Start with authentic IP. Create cultural disruption. Limited physical objects. Seek cultural validation (exhibitions, not just sales). Strategic collabs. Instagram moments.

**Labubu:** Build collector psychology (72 to collect). Mystery/reveal (blind kokoro revelation). Chase variants (special seasonal editions). Distribute strategically (hotel B2B is a unique channel).

**Agentic future:** As agents handle cognitive tasks, AUWA handles meaning. The emotional layer between human and machine. Utility apps become obsolete; relationship-based products (character, philosophy, physical objects, cultural tradition) cannot be automated away.
