Washi is what happens when mulberry bark is stripped, soaked, boiled, beaten, and suspended in mountain river water. At Shinshu Matsuzaki in Nagano, the same gesture has been practised since 1042: dip, settle, drain, turn. A thousand years of paper-making, sheet by sheet.
