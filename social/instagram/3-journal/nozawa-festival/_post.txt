On 15 January every year, the men of Nozawa Onsen build a fifteen-metre wooden shrine and then fight over whether it burns. The 25-year-olds defend the base, the 42-year-olds climb to the roof and sing as the fire climbs toward them. One of Japan's three great fire festivals, held this way since the Edo period.
