## KOKORO MIRROR — DETAILED UX FLOW

1. **Arrival:** Dark, calm screen. Soft glowing orb. Single text input (200-word limit).
2. **Input:** User types freely. Submit. Text fades.
3. **Light Shower:** Aurora-coloured particles, 3–5 seconds. Subtle audio on interaction.
4. **Kokoro Revelation:** Gradient background (1 of 4 colour families), AUWA character variant (1 of 4 hand-illustrated by Rieko), calligraphic kō stamp (current micro-season), AI-generated poetic reflection. Unique every time.
5. **Vague Input Refinement:** If broad, AUWA offers 2–3 options before full revelation.
6. **Journal Prompt:** Smart pre-populated suggestion or open prompt. Optional. One tap to save.
7. **Share:** Beautiful card (9:16 + square). One tap save to camera roll. Journal stays private.
8. **Save:** Auto-saved to Kokoro Archive.
9. **Returning Users:** "AUWA remembers you. Your last visit, you carried [emotion]."

### EQ Building (Salovey & Mayer Four Pillars)
- **Pillar 1 (Perceiving):** Kokoro revelation identifies emotion + sub-expression.
- **Pillar 2 (Using):** Journal prompt after revelation.
- **Pillar 3 (Understanding):** Kokoro Archive — visual timeline in 4 colours. After 10+ revelations: observations, not advice.
- **Pillar 4 (Managing):** Natural result of Pillars 1–3. Future: gently surface pathways. B2B opportunity.

### Freemium Model
- **Free:** Unlimited revelations, journal, archive, shareable cards.
- **Paid (£6.99/month or £49.99/year):** AI memory across sessions, activity-emotion correlation, extended archive analytics, story unlocks, early access to figure drops.

### Future Features
Activity pills (Daylio-inspired correlation), AI memory across sessions, morning/evening toggle, zoomable archive timeline, Year in Kokoro colour grid. NO streaks, NO gamification, NO push notifications, NO personality analysis.

## TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

**Stack:** Next.js (React, TypeScript), Vercel, Sanity CMS, Vercel Postgres, Claude API (Haiku 4.5), Framer Motion, Resend, Stripe.

**AI Cost Model:** Haiku 4.5 ~£0.002/revelation (with caching). Sonnet ceiling ~£0.008–0.015. At 2,000 DAU: £140–600/month API cost vs £14,000/month subscription revenue. Unit economics healthy at every stage.

**PWA rationale:** Ship in weeks, no App Store gatekeeping, no 30% commission, instant updates, same URL for everything, QR-accessible for hotel B2B. Wrappable via Capacitor later if needed.

## COMPETITIVE POSITIONING

**Category:** "Daily EQ Practice" (new category). No existing competitor combines: cultural philosophy + companion character + visual art system + shareable output + physical collectibles + four-pillar EQ framework.

**Key Competitors:**
- **Stoic** (most relevant) — philosophy-grounded, Western, no AI, no visual art
- **Rosebud** — AI emotional reflection, therapeutic chatbot, no cultural depth
- **Mindsera** — philosophy + AI, Western analytical, complex
- **Daylio** — frictionless mood tracking, no depth
- **Headspace/Calm** — stress reduction, not EQ building
- **Labubu/KAWS** — collectible figures, no narrative or digital companion

**The moat:** Features can be copied. Rieko's decade of development, native Japanese grounding, 4 illustrated books, Yaoyorozu no Kami philosophy embedded by lived experience — these cannot be prompted, researched, or bought.
