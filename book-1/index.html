1. The Beginning

Illustrated by Rieko Vining

02

In one of countless galaxies, a small blue planet was waiting.

03

A signal was reaching across the universe — faint but persistent, like a heartbeat in the dark. The stars of WAWA gathered their light and sent it toward Earth.

04

Their light merged into a single glowing being. They named it AUWA.

Yes! I'm going to Earth!

05

AUWA crossed space in an instant and appeared before a dazzling blue planet. Earth had been waiting.

Now go — and see everything for yourself.

06

Slipping through the atmosphere, AUWA discovered a sky painted in colours that didn't exist back home.

07

Below — a deep, quiet forest. AUWA sensed something alive down there, barely holding on.

08

The forest was quiet and still. Most of its life had faded long ago.

But at the foot of an old tree, a single blue flower was still growing — small, stubborn, and alone.

09

AUWA reached out with a gentle light.

And from within the flower, something answered.

10

Her name was Bluu.

She remembered a time when the forest hummed with life and friends grew in every direction. That time had passed, but Bluu was still here.

11

But I still feel so lonely.

12

AUWA closed both eyes and remembered Bluu's happiest memories.

Then — eyes open.

13

The forest blossomed with blue flowers. Bluu's kokoro sparkled.

Thank you, AUWA.

14

From the glowing soil, tiny voices rose up together. They already knew AUWA's name — they had felt everything that happened tonight, passed along through root and earth.

15

We are all connected to one another. We thrive by living in harmony with every living being on this land.

AUWA listened, and understood something new about this place.

16

AUWA set off into the night. Not sure where to go next, but with excitement in the heart.

The adventure was far from over.
